# M4 Unstructured Data Sources

## 1. GDELT 1.0 – Daily Event CSV
http://data.gdeltproject.org/events/index.html

## 2. Kaggle – Financial News NLP Dataset
https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/sayelabualigah/high-quality-financial-news-dataset-for-nlp-tasks

## 3. FNSPID – Financial News + Stock Prices
https://github.com/Zdong104/FNSPID_Financial_News_Dataset

## 4. PR Newswire – Browse & Scrape
https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/news-releases-list.page/

## 5. GlobeNewswire – RSS Feed
https://www.globenewswire.com/RssFeed/subjectcode/14-News%20Release

---

These five sources collectively cover global news events, financial news articles with sentiment labels, company-level press releases, and stock-linked news data — providing the unstructured text foundation needed for sentiment analysis, signal filtering, and market narrative tracking in the quantitative risk assessment.
